Music Recommendations from the Musicians You Love

About

Plinks is like sitting down for coffee with your favorite band and finding out what music they're really into nowadays.

In this digital age, discovering great new music is surprisingly hard. Plinks delivers easy to navigate music recommendation lists, curated by your favorite artists and bands.

A Window In

Plinks does all the sorting, instantly giving you a firsthand look into the music your idols can't get enough of.

Word of Mouth

The best recommendations come from people you trust. Who would you trust more for music ideas than your favorite bands?

No More Algorithms

It's hard to put something as personal as musical taste into rigid categories. We don't try.
