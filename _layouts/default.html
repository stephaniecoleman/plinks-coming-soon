Our team

Alexa Voytek

Co-Founder and CEO

Alexa has worked as a media relations and public affairs consultant for international corporations and foreign governments. Alexa holds a BA in History from Duke University and an MA in Russian, Eurasian and East European Studies from Columbia University.

Stephanie Coleman

Co-Founder and COO

Stephanie is a software engineer and fiddle player. She teaches web development to kids at Flatiron School, and previously worked as a producer for NPR and other national public radio outlets. She earned a BA in History and a Certificate in Documentary Studies at Duke University.

Cassandra Jenkins

Designer

Cassandra is a musician and graphic designer. She released her solo debut record in 2013, and leads her own band, as well as accompanies others as a touring bassist. She previously worked on the editorial staff of the New Yorker Magazine, and graduated from Rhode Island School of Design with a BFA in Graphic Design.
